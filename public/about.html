Description:

Savvy is a shopping application. You can store items here and allocate funds to them when able.
You can easily store items via a link or chrome extension.

Features:

  • Store items via link or extension
  • Create custom items. E.g. holiday, mortgages, etc.
  • Track current funds
  • Allocate funds to shopping items
  • An "explore" page where companies can advertise items
